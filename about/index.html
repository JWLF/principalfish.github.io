Principal Fish

I am a 26 year old maths graduate turned professional poker player turned prospective computer programmer.

Since graduation in 2009 I have made my living playing (mostly online) poker. I lived in London for 3 of those years and travelled to America to play in the World Series of Poker for 2 consecutive years.

In the last couple of years I have found poker to be less fulfilling than I'd like it to be so I'm currently focusing my efforts on learning how to code.

So far I've been focusing on Python and Javascript whilst picking up HTML/CSS along the way.

My first major project has been to create a dynamic map of constituencies in the UK using d3js. I am currently working on making an easily updatable prediction page based on current polling and possibly to make a live version for election day itself.

I have keen interests in chess, walking, playing folk music and astronomy.
