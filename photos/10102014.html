October 10th - Bynack More from Allt Mor

Region - Cairngorms.

Walk - distance 15 miles, ascent 750m.

Path took me east past Lochan Uaine before turning to the south past Bynack Stable and on to Bynack More. Return route was the same. Saw flock of 6-8 Ptarmigan around 200m below the top.
