Principal Fish

October 29th - Braeriach from Whitewell

Region - Cairngorms.

Walk - distance 16 miles, ascent 1200m.

Headed south east through the forests of Rothiemurchus to reach path up the Lairig Ghru. After a couple of miles, path branched off to right and headed steeply up the shoulder of Braeriach - Sron na Lairig. After an hour or so reached the top, very cold but stunning views. Headed off the mountain to the west, down into Glen Einich. Couldn't find path (not even sure there is one) so descent was very rough down steep heather/boulders. Reached bottom in one piece and hobbled back to car.
